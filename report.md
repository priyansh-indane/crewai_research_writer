Grand Theft Auto VI (GTA 6) is set to transport players back to a modernized Vice City, a vibrant open-world environment reminiscent of its classic predecessor from 2002. This iteration will feature multiple protagonists, allowing players to switch between characters, enriching the story and gameplay experience. An advanced dynamic weather system will introduce realistic storms and conditions, enhancing immersion and presenting unique challenges. Rockstar promises an evolving gameplay environment where player decisions significantly impact outcomes, ensuring that each playthrough feels fresh and distinct. Lastly, GTA 6 aims to expand online multiplayer, building on the success of GTA Online with ongoing support and new content post-launch.
